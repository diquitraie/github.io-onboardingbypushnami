At a glance

  • Click "Add Campaign" on your dashboard to start.
  • Name is internal only; your audience never sees it.
  • U.S. sending only; segment by State and Device (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet, Other).
  • Schedule start defaults to today; optional end date; add active days/hours as needed.
  • Times use Central Time (CST, GMT-6).
  • Set Default Bid; minimum allowed bid is $0.10.
  • Optional: Daily Spend Limit to warn as you near the cap.
  • Click "Create Campaign" to launch or pause anytime later.

What to fill in

General settings

Choose a campaign name for your own reference. It does not appear to subscribers.

Audience rules

Send within the USA only. Use dropdowns to target by state and device (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet, Other); default is All.

Schedule

Start date auto-sets to today; change it if needed. End date is optional. Add Active Hours to pick days and exact times; toggle off All Day to set start and end times. All hours are CST (GMT-6).

Budget

Default Bid is the starting bid across sites; minimum is $0.10 and can be raised or lowered later. Set a Daily Spend Limit if you want alerts as you near your budget.

Finish and launch

After those fields are set, click "Create Campaign". You can pause or adjust the campaign at any time.
